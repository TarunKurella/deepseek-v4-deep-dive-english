Against a common BF16 GQA-8 baseline (head dim = 128), the V4 family compresses the KV cache for 1M context down to roughly the 2% range.

That 2% is not the result of one isolated trick. It comes from the combination of CSA/HCA dual compression, shared-KV MQA, and FP8 main storage with BF16 positional dimensions. In plain language: compress fifty parts down to one, and million-token context moves from “data-center problem” toward “single-card feasible”.
