1M is the ceiling, not the daily workload

WHY IT MATTERS
You are not buying more words.
You are buying freedom.
Most of the time you still use only tens of thousands of tokens. But you no longer need to pre-chunk, build RAG, or prioritize context by hand. If something should go in, it goes in.

It is like plumbing: you may only drink one glass of water a day, but you still want running water at home because when you need it, you cannot improvise.
The commercial value of 1M is not merely “it fits.” It is the marginal disappearance of planning overhead. That is a key threshold for moving agents from demos into production.
