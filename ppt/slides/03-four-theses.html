Chapter 0 · Four claims that organize the whole reading

Before you read V4, remember these four things

THESIS 01
Not world-best,
but 1M Agent access for everyone
V4 is not a “break the ceiling” flagship launch. It is the first time 1M-token context + Agent capability has been packaged into something ordinary users can afford to use by default.
See Chapter 9
THESIS 02
Competition-programming DNA,
strong at problem solving
Most of the DeepSeek team comes from competition backgrounds, and the model reflects that. On math and coding tasks with clear, verifiable answers, it is the current open-model ceiling.
See Chapter 7
THESIS 03
Still behind on
taste-heavy tasks
Its creative-writing win rate trails Claude Opus 4.5, let alone 4.6 or 4.7. The paper openly admits this in its limitations section. Standardized tasks are a strength; open-ended taste tasks are not.
See Chapter 8
THESIS 04
Overall, the model is unusually honest and disciplined
The paper openly admits mechanisms that work without full explanation, and the pricing page explicitly says Pro should get cheaper after Ascend 950 supply ramps. No hype, no hiding, no empty promises.
See Chapter 10
