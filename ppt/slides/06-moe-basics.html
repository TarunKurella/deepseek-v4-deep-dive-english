Chapter 1 · What MoE really means

A 1.6T-parameter model that invites only 6 experts to speak each step

"The essence of MoE is an expert meeting, not a full-team vote.
1.6T parameters describe the size of the team. The 49B activated per token are the experts who actually speak on that step.
Routing: each V4-Pro layer has 384 routed experts plus 1 shared expert. For each token, the gating network selects the 6 most relevant routed experts. The first three layers use hash routing to stabilize early training; later layers use a Sigmoid → Sqrt(Softplus) scoring function.
Deployment cost: you still need the full 1.6T model resident because any token may hit different experts, but each forward step computes only about 49B active parameters.
