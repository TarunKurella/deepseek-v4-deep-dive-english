CHAPTER 02 · THE RESIDUAL BOTTLENECK

As depth grows, the signal distorts in transit

Residual connections work because they let information skip layers. But once the network goes past 60 layers, the skip path itself starts accumulating noise and amplification so gradients either vanish or explode.
The limitation of the standard setup: classic residual links implicitly give every bypass path weight 1, as if every layer mattered equally. In reality some layers matter more than others, and unconstrained accumulation pushes very deep models into an unstable regime.
DeepSeek-V4’s move: do not replace residual connections, but add a mathematical guardrail on top of them: Manifold-Constrained Hyper-Connections, or mHC.
