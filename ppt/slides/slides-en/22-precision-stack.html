Use fewer bits, but only where fewer bits are safe

The rougher the task, the fewer bits it gets. The more delicate the step, the higher the precision stays. The result is one attention stack running three numeric formats at once.

The selection philosophy behind mixed precision
Not every number inside attention matters equally. Scoring can be coarse, as long as the final compute stays precise. That is why V4 pushes the indexer down to FP4 while keeping core attention at BF16.
