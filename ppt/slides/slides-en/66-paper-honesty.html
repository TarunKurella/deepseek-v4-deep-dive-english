CHAPTER 10 · THESIS 4 · HONESTY AND DISCIPLINE

Inside the paper: three “we still do not understand this” admissions

Section 6, Limitations. These are weaknesses DeepSeek chooses to write down itself. That level of candor is rare in LLM papers.
01
Architecture · Too many components
A lot of validated pieces
got stacked together
“We retained many preliminarily validated components and tricks, which, while effective, made the architecture relatively complex.”
To reduce training risk, they kept layering on many validated tricks. The next version needs subtraction, not more accumulation.
02
Stability · Mechanism unclear
It works,
but they do not know exactly why
“Anticipatory Routing and SwiGLU Clamping ... their underlying principles remain insufficiently understood.”
Expert pre-routing and activation clamping do help, but the mechanism is still not fully understood. Writing that plainly in the paper is unusual honesty for this field.
03
Sparsity · Still not extreme enough
They have not hit
the sparsity ceiling yet
“Beyond MoE and sparse attention ... explore new dimensions of sparsity.”
MoE and sparse attention are only the start. They want to push sparsity across more dimensions. V5 will likely move toward scalable lookup-style memory modules.
Most papers try to prove they are flawless. DeepSeek’s paper tries to tell you what is still unfinished. That is engineering language, not marketing language.
