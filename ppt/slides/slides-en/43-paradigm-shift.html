06 · PARADIGM SHIFT
SUMMARY · WHY THIS COUNTS AS A PARADIGM SHIFT

RL’s role gets redefined

In the old framing, RL was the final mile for aligning the final model to human preference. In V4, RL becomes an intermediate step used to train specialists, while the final student only distills.
Dimension
Traditional SFT + RLHF
DeepSeek V4 two-stage
Multi-task conflict
Joint optimization makes tasks interfere, and reward weights become an endless tug-of-war
Physical isolation, so experts do not interfere with one another
RL stability
The student runs RL directly, so curves shake and runs crash often
RL is confined to the specialist stage, while the student only distills
Adding new capability
You retune reward balance and risk hurting existing skills
Add one new expert, and the path stays clean
Infrastructure bar
Moderate
Extremely high: full-vocab distillation + multi-teacher scheduling
Author’s View
Use engineering to route around RL instability: instead of making one universal model fight inside a messy reward landscape, train several specialized models on simpler landscapes and let the final student learn from them. That is deeper than any single architecture tweak.
