CHAPTER 03 · CSA · COMPRESSED SPARSE ATTENTION

Merge 64 tokens into one block, then read only the most relevant blocks closely

Compression plus sparsity gives two rounds of dimensionality reduction. The Lightning Indexer does the scoring and only lets important blocks into core attention.
STEP 01 · COMPRESS
Merge every 64 tokens into one entry
Use learned compression weights to form weighted sums, shrinking the sequence to n / m. Two KV tracks, Cᵃ and Cᵇ, plus overlapping stride preserve boundary information.
STEP 02 · INDEX
Score with the Lightning Indexer
A low-rank projection produces the indexer query, which computes an index score It,s for every compressed block. FP4 is used here to buy speed at the cost of a tiny precision hit.
STEP 03 · SELECT
Only top-k enters core attention
The query selects the top-k compressed blocks and runs DSA, DeepSeek Sparse Attention, only on them. Compression gives you a 1/m factor, and sparsity adds another k / (n/m) reduction.
