CHAPTER 10 · THESIS 4 · HONESTY AND DISCIPLINE

The V5 roadmap: they tell you directly what comes next

The final paragraph of Section 6 lays out five future directions. Where others hide the roadmap, DeepSeek prints it plainly.
01
More extreme sparsity
Not just sparse MoE and sparse attention, but new dimensions of sparsity.
02
Lower-latency architecture
Make long-context deployment more responsive and better suited for agent interaction.
03
Long-horizon agents
Multi-turn, multi-hour long-duration tasks as a primary next target.
04
Multimodality
V5 will likely be natively multimodal rather than adapter-driven.
05
Synthetic data and curation
Keep improving the data pipeline, especially the quality of synthetic data.
V5 PREVIEW · THE KEY SIGNAL
The references point to Cheng et al. 2026 on “Conditional Memory via Scalable Lookup”.
That strongly suggests V5 may introduce a scalable lookup-based memory module that moves beyond attention alone.
