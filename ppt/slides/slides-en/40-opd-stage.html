STAGE 02 · ON-POLICY DISTILLATION

More than ten experts become teachers, one student gets distilled

The student samples its own trajectories, then aligns against all experts with reverse KL. This is DeepSeek’s new multi-teacher distillation game.
Objective
ℒOPD(θ) = Σi=1..N  wi · DKL( πθ ‖ πEi )
REVERSE KL
The student converges
to high-probability regions
Forward KL pushes the student to cover every mode of the teachers and often produces an over-smoothed compromise. Reverse KL concentrates the student into the high-probability regions, so learning becomes sharper and more decisive.
ON-POLICY
The student samples
its own trajectories
Sampling does not come from the teachers. It comes from the student’s own policy. That means the student always trains on the distribution it will actually encounter, rather than on teacher outputs it might never reach by itself.
FULL-VOCAB LOGITS
Use the full logit vector
to compute exact KL
Earlier work often approximated KL with sampling to save compute. V4 keeps the full logit distribution, which gives lower gradient variance and more stable training. The price is much higher compute, and infrastructure has to absorb that cost.
