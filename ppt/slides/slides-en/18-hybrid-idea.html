CHAPTER 03 · HYBRID ATTENTION CSA + HCA

Two filters, one coarse and one fine

Anyone who researches for real knows the pattern: first scan the table of contents to find the right section, then read one or two paragraphs closely. V4 turns that workflow into the attention stack itself.
FINE FILTER
CSA Compressed Sparse Attention
Compress + sparsify to locate the critical tokens
It compresses KV into blocks at granularity m = 64, then uses a Lightning Indexer to score those blocks and only sends the top-k most relevant ones into the expensive path.
COARSE FILTER
HCA Heavily Compressed Attention
Aggressive compression, but global coverage
It compresses the sequence much more aggressively at m′ ≫ m, but does not sparsify afterward. Every query still scans all compressed blocks densely. The granularity gets coarse, but the global view survives.
