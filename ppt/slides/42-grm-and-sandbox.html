For tasks that are hard to evaluate with explicit rules, like writing quality or helpfulness, the traditional approach is a scalar reward model. V4 goes the other direction: use a generative model as the judge, and optimize the GRM itself with RL. Evaluation quality and generation quality improve together.
Judge and policy are unified, which points in the same direction as Anthropic’s Constitutional AI and helps resist reward hacking.
